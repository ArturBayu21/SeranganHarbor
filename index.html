Our Services

Moring

Moring has the same function as the anchor except that the moring has a higher level of safetines because it is heavier. at our harbour, we recommend boats to use moring instead of anchor because our seabed is mud.

Safety

We work closely with maritime security to ensure the safety of our harbor. Every week we carry out routine patrols to make sure everything is under control.

Schoci

Schoci is a small boat that you will use to go to the coast or land from where your boat is anchored or docked. This small boat is very useful when you want to visit your boat from mainland or you just want to go around whereever you want.
